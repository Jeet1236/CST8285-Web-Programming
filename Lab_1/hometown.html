Welcome to Gandhinagar

City Information

  • State: Gujarat
  • Country: India
  • Population: 206,167

Gandhinagar is the capital of Gujarat. It is called the green city of India. Akshardham temple is the famous temple which attracts lots of tourists to this place. This city is structured in the smart way. The areas areas are called sectors and there are roads alphabetically. Gandhinagar can be navigated logically.

Major Festivals of Gandhinagar

  1. Navratri
  2. Diwali
  3. Makar Sakranti(Kite flying)
  4. Raksha Bandhann
